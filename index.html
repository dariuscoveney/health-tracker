Connect your Google Sheet

Your data lives in a Google Sheet you control — visible, editable, and accessible from any device.

1Create a new Google Sheet and name it (e.g. "Health tracker — [your name]").
2Open Extensions → Apps Script. Click "Untitled project" at the top and rename it to Health Tracker. Then delete all existing code and paste in the apps_script.js file provided alongside this file.
3Click Deploy → New deployment. Select type: Web app. Set "Execute as" to Me and "Who has access" to Anyone. Click Deploy and copy the URL that appears.
4Authorise the script. Paste the URL into a browser tab and press enter. Click Review Permissions, select your Google account, then click Advanced → Go to Health Tracker (unsafe) and click Allow. You should see raw data in the browser — that means it’s working.
5Come back here, paste the deployment URL below, click Test then Connect.
